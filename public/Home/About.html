Our Story

We Believe Every Book Deserves a New Reader

BookBridge was built to make books accessible to everyone in Pakistan — whether you want to buy, sell, borrow, donate, or exchange, we've got you covered.

Our Mission

Connecting Readers Across Pakistan

Millions of books sit on shelves unread. At the same time, millions of students and readers can't afford the books they need. BookBridge bridges that gap with a trusted, verified community where books find new homes.

Every listing, every exchange, every donated book is a step toward a more literate Pakistan.
