Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about BookBridge.

General
BookBridge is Pakistan's first complete book ecosystem where users can buy, sell, borrow, donate, and exchange books with verified readers across the country.
Creating an account and browsing books is completely free. BookBridge charges a small service fee only on successful paid transactions.
Simply create a free account, complete your profile, and start browsing or listing books. It takes less than 2 minutes to sign up.
Buying & Selling
Browse books, find one you like, and click 'Chat to Buy' to message the seller directly. Negotiate price and arrange payment through our secure system.
After logging in, click 'List a Book', fill in the details, upload photos, set your price, and publish. Your book goes live instantly.
Payments are handled securely through our platform. We support wallet transfers and will be integrating Stripe for card payments soon.
Borrowing
Find a book listed as 'Borrow', click 'Request to Borrow', set the duration (up to the owner's maximum), and send a message. The owner approves or rejects your request.
You'll receive overdue alerts and the owner will be notified. Repeated late returns may affect your rating and ability to borrow in the future.
Some owners may require a refundable security deposit which is returned when you return the book on time.
Safety & Trust
Users can submit their CNIC number for identity verification. Verified users display a blue checkmark badge on their profile, building trust in the community.
BookBridge has a fraud monitoring system and you can report any suspicious activity. Verified identities mean scammers can be held accountable.
After a transaction, both parties can leave a star rating and written review. These reviews build each user's reputation on the platform.

Our support team is here to help you get the most out of BookBridge.
